Warehouse Master

Centralized Inventory & Data Management for WordPress Online Stores

1. Product Overview

Warehouse Master is a SaaS platform that helps businesses manage products, inventory, and orders across multiple WordPress (WooCommerce) online stores from a single, centralized admin portal.

Instead of logging into multiple WordPress dashboards, users can operate all connected stores through one independent management system.

3. Relationship Between Laravel & WordPress

Warehouse Master does not replace WordPress.

WordPress is used as the storefront for products, checkout, and payments.

Laravel acts as the central admin system for inventory, synchronization, and reporting.

The two systems communicate only via secure APIs, ensuring isolation and scalability.
